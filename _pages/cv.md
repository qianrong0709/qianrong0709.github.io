Research Profile
======
I am a PhD student working on AI for Drug Discovery (AIDD), with a current focus on Molecular Generation. My research interests center on generative models that incorporate physical knowledge, molecular structure, and lower-level molecular information such as Electronic Density and dynamic molecular processes.

Before entering AIDD, I was trained in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Knowledge Graphs. This background motivates my interest in connecting symbolic knowledge, learned representations, and scientific modeling for AI for Science.

Research Experience and Training
======
<ul class="compact-list">
  <li><strong>Molecular Generation:</strong> Interested in generative models for molecular design, including approaches informed by physical constraints, molecular geometry, and chemical validity.</li>
  <li><strong>Physical and Structural Modeling:</strong> Exploring how lower-level molecular information, such as Electronic Density and dynamic molecular behavior, can guide generation and representation learning.</li>
  <li><strong>Protein and Molecular Systems:</strong> Broader interests include Protein Design, protein-ligand interaction modeling, and representation learning for molecular systems.</li>
